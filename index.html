Projects

Protective effect of by-products of the dairy industry during the biotechnological production of lactic acid bacteria.

The influence of by-products such as sour whey on the growth of Lactobacillus brevis and Lactobacillus plantarum bacteria was investigated. Cultivation techniques, fermentative activity of bacteria and antioxidant properties were analyzed. The laboratory methods used were bacterial cell cultivation, indirect cell counting, measurement of pH and lactic acid percentage, lyophilization procedure, SDS-PAGE electrophoresis, removal of S-protein, centrifugation, testing of antioxidant activity and application of aseptic work technique.
